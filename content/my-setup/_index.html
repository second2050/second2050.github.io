My Hardware:

On the go

As my portable workstation I use the, already pretty old, Lenovo ThinkPad T440p. Mine is equipped with an Intel Core i7 4710MQ, 12GB of RAM and the dedicated Nvidia GeForce GT730M + the 1080p IPS display. It also got the LTE modem and Smartcard reader included

I flashed an unlocked BIOS (Guide by Wolfgang) on it and upgraded the WiFi card to an Intel AX210. The CPU is also undervolted for better battery life which more or less works.

The unlocked BIOS might be replaced with Libreboot in the future.
